About us

More than 30 years experience

Our business is family owned for more than 30 years and has had no shortage of experienced and highly motivated contracters willing to serve you and your construction needs. Starting as a humble carpet store, we have recieved great experience in terms of renovation, repairs and additions, giving us an A+ rating by the better business bureau. We are a company based in Contra Costa County, and we are available to work throughout the Bay Area, including San Francisco, Oakland and San Jose.

Our Team

Comfort Construction specializes in indoor living spaces with focus on creating the perfect interior design that you have only dreamed of. We can effectively undertake any large scale custom residential or commercial project, at the same time doing small projects with the same professionalism and quality. Our professional team of designers and highly skilled construction team have the expertise and experience to manage your project.

Our Mission

We strive to make a difference in the lives of our employees, customers and community. Fundamental to Comfort Construction's reputation is providing high quality construction services on time and within budget, and has been elemental to maintaining long-lasting relationships with clients, subcontractors, architects, consultants and lenders, many of which go back several years.

Our Values

We recognize our primary asset is people, therefore provide opportunity and encouragement to help our people reach their potential. We work with our clients as a team. Mutual respect provides the foundation for our success. We are proactive in finding solutions for our clients that best achieve their goals. We understand that lasting relationships are the lifeblood of our business so we pay personal attention to our clients by focusing on them as individuals.
